Why we built it

Most tracking apps ask too much. We ask for a photo.

Students eat at the Mensa every day with no idea what's on their plate — and the apps that could help take so many taps that most people quit within a week.

The old way

  • No idea what's actually in your Mensa meal
  • Too many steps — people give up in days
  • Generic goals that ignore your body
  • No guidance on what to eat next

The EasyFit way

  • A calorie goal built from your own metrics
  • Log any meal by photo — AI does the rest
  • TH Augsburg Mensa menu, built in
  • Smart suggestions for your remaining budget

What's inside

Everything you need, nothing you don't

AI meal scan

Point your camera at a plate. The AI recognises the food and estimates portion sizes for you.

Mensa integration

The TH Augsburg cafeteria menu is updated daily, so a campus lunch logs in just two taps.

Personal calorie goal

A goal tailored to your age, weight, height and activity — recalculated whenever your stats change.

Progress charts

Watch calorie and macro trends across days, weeks, months and the whole year.

Smart suggestions

EasyFit recommends meals that fit the calories and macros you have left for the day.

Apple Health

On iOS, EasyFit reads your activity and writes nutrition records straight to HealthKit.

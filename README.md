# EasyFit — Landing Page

The marketing and download page for **EasyFit**, an AI-powered nutrition tracker.
Snap a photo of your meal and EasyFit works out what it is and what's in it —
Mensa meals log in two taps.

Built as a 4th-semester project at the **Technische Hochschule Augsburg**,
Faculty of Computer Science (SS 2026).

---

## About the app

Tracking food shouldn't feel like a second job. Most nutrition apps ask for so many
taps that people quit within a week, and they leave students guessing about what's
actually on their Mensa plate. EasyFit replaces all of that with a photo.

**Key features:**
- **AI meal scan** — point the camera at a plate; the AI recognises the food and estimates portions.
- **Mensa integration** — the TH Augsburg cafeteria menu is updated daily, so a campus lunch logs in two taps.
- **Personal calorie goal** — tailored to your age, weight, height and activity, recalculated as your stats change.
- **Progress charts** — calorie and macro trends across days, weeks, months and the year.
- **Smart suggestions** — meal ideas that fit the calories and macros you have left for the day.
- **Apple Health** — on iOS, reads activity and writes nutrition records to HealthKit.

---

## About this page

A single, self-contained landing page that introduces EasyFit and lets visitors
download the Android app directly while it's in beta (not yet on the Play Store).

It's a static page — plain HTML and CSS, no build step and no frameworks. Fonts are
loaded from Google Fonts; everything else is inline. The design uses a warm,
student-friendly palette with the app's calorie-ring motif echoed throughout
(step bubbles, section marks).

**Sections:** hero with a phone mockup · problem vs. solution · how it works in five
steps · feature grid · project stats · download · team footer.
